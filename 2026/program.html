This symposium is a part of a 1-month open research residency (see 2025 occurence). This program was heavily inspired, and aims to continue the legacy of:

  • The Hacker Trip To China by Noisebridge, the San Francisco Hackerspace.
  • The Research At Scale program by the MIT Media Lab.
